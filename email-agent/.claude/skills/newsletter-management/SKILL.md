# Newsletter Management Skill

## Newsletter Sources
techcrunch.com, morningbrew.com, hackernews.com, substack.com

## Archival Policy
- Archive newsletter older than 30 days
- NEver archive with "important" label or starred

## Workflow
1. Search `label:newsletter older_than:30d`
2. Archive in batches
3. Report count
